Principal Talk EDUCATION IS THE MOST POWERFUL THING YOU CAN USE TO CHANGE THE WORLD.

At St. Ann’s Public School, we believe in nurturing not just minds, but also dreams. Our students are creative, dedicated, and driven and we empower them to turn ideas into action through real, hands-on opportunities.

"The task of the modern educator is not to cut down jungles, but to irrigate deserts."

Mrs. Cony Chattree St Ann's Public School Principal

We have faith in our student future

At St. Ann’s Public School, we believe that every child is born with unique gifts waiting to be discovered and nurtured. Our role is not just to teach, but to empower — to create an environment where students feel confident, capable, and courageous. We trust in their creativity, respect their individuality, and walk alongside them as they grow into thoughtful, responsible, and impactful individuals. Because when we believe in them, they learn to believe in themselves.

Empower

At St. Ann’s, we believe that true education begins with empowerment.
We equip students with the tools, confidence, and courage to think, lead, and create. Every lesson is an opportunity to build leaders for tomorrow.

Engage

Learning comes alive when students are engaged.
Our classrooms foster curiosity, participation, and hands-on experiences that spark deeper understanding. We don’t just teach — we involve, inspire, and ignite.

Graduation

Graduation is not the end, it’s the beginning of purpose.
Our students leave with more than just certificates; they carry values, vision, and confidence. St. Ann’s graduates are prepared to step into the world and make a difference.

Awarding

Recognition is part of how we celebrate growth.
From academic excellence to character and creativity, we honour every milestone. At St. Ann’s, each award is a reflection of hard work, heart, and dedication.
